SUMMARY: JJ VEGA'S COACHING PROFILE
======================================================================

PRIMARY FOCUSES AS A COACH
--------------------------

1. Leadership Development & Team Dynamics
   The dominant theme by far. JJ spends the most time helping clients
   navigate their roles as leaders -- managing teams, having difficult
   conversations, building trust within organizations, and stepping
   into leadership with greater confidence and intentionality.

2. Body-Based / Somatic Awareness
   A signature element of JJ's approach. References to the body,
   nervous system, energy, and somatic awareness appear consistently
   across sessions. JJ brings clients back to their physical
   experience as a way to access deeper awareness and break reactive
   patterns.

3. Goal Setting & Action Planning
   JJ is highly action-oriented. Sessions frequently center on
   defining goals, creating plans, identifying next steps, and
   establishing accountability. He balances depth work with practical
   forward movement.

4. Work-Life Integration
   Family, kids, marriage, and personal life show up almost as much
   as professional topics. JJ doesn't silo coaching into "just work"
   -- he treats the whole person, addressing how career pressures
   bleed into home life and vice versa.

5. Relational Skills & Communication
   Conversations, feedback, boundaries, listening, and conflict
   resolution are recurring themes. JJ helps clients improve how they
   communicate -- both with teams at work and with partners/family at
   home.

6. Identity, Vision & Values
   JJ frequently works in the space of purpose, mission, core values,
   and "who am I." He helps clients get clear on what they stand for
   and align their actions with their deeper identity.


CHALLENGES JJ HELPS CLIENTS NAVIGATE
-------------------------------------

- Navigating leadership transitions -- stepping into bigger roles,
  leading teams for the first time, or redefining their leadership
  style

- Setting and holding boundaries -- both professionally (with teams,
  bosses, organizations) and personally (with family, partners)

- Processing fear, anger, and frustration -- emotional patterns that
  show up in decision-making and relationships

- Overcoming burnout and restoring balance -- managing energy, rest,
  and sustainability in demanding roles

- Building confidence and trust -- both self-trust and building trust
  with teams

- Breaking reactive patterns -- using body awareness and triggers as
  doorways to more intentional responses

- Aligning professional ambitions with personal values -- career
  decisions, entrepreneurship, and finding meaning in work
